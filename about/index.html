What is Unfold?

At its core, Unfold is a web series about people, their culture, and the places that culture resides.

Unfold is a project we would like to bring around the world - learning about different cultures and the way people enjoy their cities. Each episode should feel true to the locals, capturing the essence of their home, while delighting and inspiring viewers. Travel shows tend to focus on food or landmarks, while Unfold seeks to find the true meaning of a city - why it matters to be there.

We felt there was a need for accessible, high quality video content, and we want to produce it. The way we consume and encounter new content is changing, Unfold hopes to be a part of that. We hope you’ll let us.
